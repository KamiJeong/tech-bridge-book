## Question Pattern

- **Reflection question**: [Open-ended question]

## Required Reflection Dimensions

- What did I want Claude to help me decide or create?
- What context did Claude need that I did not provide at first?
- What might Claude misunderstand?
- Which parts need verification?
- What information should not be shared?
- Is the output usable as-is or does it need human editing?
- How could the prompt or answer be improved?
- What human judgment is still required?
