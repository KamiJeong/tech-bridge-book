# Definition of Done: Using Claude

A lesson is complete only when all three language versions, exercises, thinking questions, safety review, and next steps are present.

## Per-Lesson Completion Checklist

- [ ] Korean source file exists at the correct mirrored path.
- [ ] English translation exists and follows the same structure.
- [ ] Japanese translation exists and follows the same structure.
- [ ] All required lesson sections are present and non-empty.
- [ ] At least one Claude prompt example is included.
- [ ] At least one hands-on exercise can be completed immediately.
- [ ] At least one thinking question asks for learner judgment.
- [ ] Safety checklist items match the lesson scenario.
- [ ] Plan-dependent Claude features include an availability note.
- [ ] Recurring terms are represented in the glossary.
- [ ] Human review verifies accuracy, tone, privacy, and meaning preservation.
- [ ] SUMMARY.md links to the relevant page.

## Course-Level Completion Evidence

- Course overview exists in Korean, English, and Japanese.
- Curriculum map includes all 17 chapters in all three languages.
- Lesson, exercise, and thinking-question templates exist in all three languages.
- Prompt pattern library includes at least 13 named patterns.
- Safety checklist includes all required privacy and verification caution areas.
- Glossary includes at least 15 aligned terms.
- GitBook navigation links resolve from `SUMMARY.md`.
