# Claude Safety Checklist

Use this checklist before sharing information with Claude and before using Claude output in real work.

## Checklist

- Do not blindly trust Claude; verify important claims.
- Do not paste passwords, API keys, credentials, or access tokens.
- Do not share private customer data, personal identifiers, contact details, or payment information.
- Do not share confidential company strategy or internal documents unless policy permits it.
- Do not use Claude as the only basis for legal, medical, financial, or safety decisions.
- Do not assume facts are current; check dates and sources.
- Do not submit Claude output as final work without human review.
- Do not use Claude to avoid thinking; use it to think better.
- Avoid tasks where you cannot reasonably verify the answer.
- Before uploading files, check permission, retention policy, and sharing rules.

## File Upload Rule

Feature availability may vary by Claude plan and interface. Learners should check their current Claude screen.

Before using output in real work, a human must review facts, context, tone, and sensitive information.

## Lesson Integration

Every lesson must include at least two safety items relevant to that lesson's scenario.
