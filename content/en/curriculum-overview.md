# Curriculum Overview

Tech Bridge Book moves from simple concepts to practical work use.

| Stage | Topic | Goal |
|-------|-------|------|
| 1 | Basic digital literacy | Use files, browsers, documents, and spreadsheets with confidence. |
| 2 | AI fundamentals | Separate what AI can do from what it cannot do reliably. |
| 3 | Prompting and collaboration | Write clear requests to get useful AI output. |
| 4 | Data and documents | Use AI safely for tables, reports, summaries, and organization. |
| 5 | Automation and workflows | Break repeated work into steps and judge what can be automated. |
| 6 | Practical mini-projects | Solve a small work problem from start to finish. |
| 7 | Real-world application patterns | Apply learned methods across work situations. |

Every lesson includes explanation, example, exercise, thinking question, common mistakes, summary, and next step.
