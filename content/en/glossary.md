## Starter Terms

### AI

- Korean: 인공지능
- English: artificial intelligence
- Japanese: 人工知能
- Plain meaning: Technology that helps with tasks such as judgment, generation, and classification.
- Work example: It can summarize meeting notes or draft an email.
- Related terms: prompt, data, automation
- Translation note: Explain the abbreviation and full term when first introduced.

### Prompt

- Korean: 프롬프트
- English: prompt
- Japanese: プロンプト
- Plain meaning: The request you give to an AI tool.
- Work example: "Summarize this report in five sentences."
- Related terms: AI collaboration, context, output format
- Translation note: When needed, explain it as "a request to AI."
