# AI Safety and Privacy

AI is useful, but it is not always correct. A person should check results before using them for work.

## Core Principles

- AI answers can include hallucination.
- Personal data, customer data, company secrets, contracts, financial data, medical data, and other sensitive information must be protected.
- When using copyrighted material or AI-generated output, check sources and usage rights.
- Important decisions need human judgment and review.

## Safe Practice Data

Lessons and exercises should not use real customer names, contact details, internal documents, or private numbers. Use fictional or anonymized examples when practice data is needed.

## Check Questions

- Does this material include personal data or company secrets?
- Would I notice if the AI answer was wrong?
- Do I have a standard for checking the result before using it?
