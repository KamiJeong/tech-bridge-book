# Introduction

This book is for people who are learning AI and practical technology for the first time.

Technical words are explained in plain language, with examples and workplace situations. Examples should connect to documents, spreadsheets, reports, planning, customer messages, marketing, operations, research, and automation.

## What This Book Covers

- Basic digital literacy
- AI concepts and limits
- Prompting and AI collaboration
- Documents and data
- Repeated work and automation
- Practical mini-projects

## What Is Not Included Yet

This initial structure does not include finished lesson text. Full lessons will be added later through topic-specific specs and review.
