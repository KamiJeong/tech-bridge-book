# Tech Bridge Book

Tech Bridge Book is a GitBook document collection for non-developers who want to learn AI and practical technology from beginner basics to real work use.

This first structure is not a full lesson set. It defines the learning path, language folders, terminology practice, safety baseline, and authoring templates before lesson writing begins.

## Prerequisite Before Reading

This book is written for non-developer learners. The goal is to build your own judgment, not to memorize quick answers. Plan for about 10–20 minutes of focused time per chapter.

- Be comfortable with a web browser, a document editor, and a spreadsheet at a basic level.
- Be able to picture real work situations such as documents, reports, scheduling, and customer communication.
- Do not paste company secrets, customer data, or personal information into AI tools as-is. Read [AI Safety and Privacy](ai-safety-privacy.md) first for the details.
- Try to ask *why* something works rather than memorizing the answer.

When you are ready, open [Introduction](introduction.md) to begin.
